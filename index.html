montyhallr

A plugin that allows you to easily add the Monty Hall game to your site.

If you have not heard of the Monty Hall problem, please click here

In this version, the aim is to try to select the Pacman card. All but one of the remaining cards (with ghosts behind them) will be upturned and then you have the choice of picking the same card or switching for the final move.

Obviously, with more cards, the chances of getting the Pacman card by not switching are very small.

By playing with large amounts of cards, it is easy to make it more intuitive about how switching increases your chances of winning in the Monty Hall problem.
